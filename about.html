🏥 Our Mission

Empowering Health Decisions with AI

Kenko AI was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to intelligent healthcare—regardless of geography, income, or medical literacy.

Our Story

In many parts of the world, people wait months for basic specialist consultations. Prescriptions get lost. Medical records are scattered across clinics. Emergency symptoms go unrecognized until it's too late.

Kenko AI was founded to change this. We combine artificial intelligence with medical expertise to create a health companion that's always available—analyzing symptoms, organizing records, and connecting patients with the right care at the right time.

Our vision: A world where no health question goes unanswered, and no patient navigates their journey alone.
